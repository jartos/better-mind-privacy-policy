2. Data Subjects

This Privacy Policy applies to users who install and use the Better Mind app. The app can be used anonymously without creating an account.

3. Data We Process and Sources of Data

3.1 Personal Data and Other Information

The Better Mind app does not collect or store the following information on servers controlled by us:

  • Name, email address or username
  • Passwords or other login credentials
  • Profile information (such as age group or preferences)
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Push notification tokens
  • Journal entries, notes or similar content in any cloud service
  • Analytics data about how you use the app (for example, screen views or button clicks) tied to your identity

The app does not require you to create a user account and does not send personally identifiable information about you to our own backend or cloud services.

Content created within the app (for example, any entries or settings) is stored only on your own device. This local data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app or clear the app’s data from your device. We do not have access to this local data.

3.2 Payment-Related Information

Better Mind offers paid features (for example, subscriptions or one-time purchases). Payments and purchase validation are handled by the following third‑party services:

  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC)
  • Apple Pay / App Store (Apple Inc.)
  • RevenueCat, Inc.

Actual payment information (such as credit or debit card numbers) is processed by Google, Apple and/or RevenueCat in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies. We do not store or have access to your full payment card details.

We may receive from these providers certain technical information about your purchases, such as:

  • Product or subscription type (for example, product ID)
  • Subscription status (active / expired)
  • Technical identifiers that allow us to link purchases to a device or anonymous user identifier

We use this information only to:

  • verify that your purchase or subscription is valid; and
  • enable the corresponding premium features in the app.

We do not use this information for direct marketing or profiling.

4. Purposes and Legal Bases for Processing

The processing of personal data in connection with Better Mind is very limited and mainly concerns the technical handling of purchases via third‑party providers.

We process data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Better Mind app
  • To technically enable and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions (via Google Play Billing, Apple Pay / App Store and RevenueCat)
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and prevent misuse or fraud

Under GDPR, the main legal bases for processing are:

  • Performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR): to provide access to premium content and functionality that you purchase.
  • Legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR): some purchase-related data may be retained by third‑party providers (such as Google or Apple) to comply with accounting or other statutory requirements.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): ensuring the proper operation of the app, preventing misuse and resolving technical issues.

If your local law provides other or additional legal grounds, we rely on those to the extent they apply.

5. Data Retention

Because we do not store your personal data in our own systems, retention mainly concerns:

  1. Local data on your device
    Content and settings within the Better Mind app are stored only on your device. This data is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear the app’s data from your device. We have no access to this data.
  2. Purchase and subscription information at Google, Apple and RevenueCat
    These providers may retain information in accordance with their own terms and applicable laws (for example, for accounting or fraud prevention). We only access the information necessary to verify your entitlement to use premium features (for example, whether your subscription is active).
